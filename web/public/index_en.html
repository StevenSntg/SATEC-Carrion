Neural network versus the decision tree

On new data, the neural network achieves the best balance. The pruned tree follows closely; the same tree unpruned memorizes the past and collapses in AUC-PR, the signature of overfitting.

Pruning the tree triples its F1 (0.22 → 0.60) and multiplies its AUC-PR ninefold: limiting complexity is decisive for detecting the rare class. Even so, the neural network keeps the edge, and both outperform the classical endemic channel that guides surveillance today.
